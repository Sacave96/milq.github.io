Healthy living

These are my personal healthy living tips. Any advices or recommendations will be welcome.

HEALTHY FOOD

Drinks

  • Water.

Grains

  • Brown rice.
  • Whole wheat bread (with nuts and seeds).

Fruits

  • Bananas
  • Oranges
  • Peaches
  • Pears.
  • Strawberries.
  • Watermelon.

Poultry

  • Chicken breast.
  • Turkey breast.
  • Eggs and dairy: fortified skimmed milk, low-fat strawberry yogurt, pasteurized egg whites.
  • Seafood: hake, hake roe, salmon, sardines, shrimp, tuna.
  • Vegetables: broccoli, carrots, extra virgin olive oil, garlic, green beans (cut), green bell peppers, green peas, onions, romaine lettuce, tomatoes, radishes, spinach, swiss chard, white button mushrooms.
