Healthy living

These are my personal healthy living tips. Any advices or recommendations will be welcome.

  • Drinks: water.
  • Eggs and dairy: fortified skimmed milk, low-fat strawberry yogurt, pasteurized egg whites.
  • Fruits: bananas, oranges, peaches, pears, strawberries, watermelon.
  • Poultry and meats: chicken breast, turkey breast.
  • Seafood: hake, hake roe, salmon, sardines, shrimp, tuna.
  • Vegetables: broccoli, carrots, extra virgin olive oil, garlic, green beans (cut), green bell peppers, green peas, mushrooms, onions, romaine lettuce, spinach.
