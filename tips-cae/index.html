2. WRITING

2.0. Essay

  • I should/would strongly recommend...

2.1. Formal letter

  • I am writing with regard/reference to...
  • With this letter I hereby should/would like to...
  • With reference to your letter concerning...
  • We/I should/would like to express our/my deepest gratitude and sincere appreciation (for your support and encouragement / for the outstanding services that we/I received / to our/my advisor/parents, for their/his/her patience, sacrifice, and unlimited support).
  • I should also like to point out that...
  • I should/would be grateful if you could (kindly)...
  • Should you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.
  • I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.
  • Yours sincerely, (if you know the name of the recipient).
  • Yours faithfully, (if you don’t know the name of the recipient).

2.2. Informal letter

  • Looking forward to hearing from you.
  • Can't wait to see you again.
  • All the best, (to friends).
  • All my love forever, (to close friends or relatives).
