2. WRITING

2.1. Formal letter

  • I am writing with regard/reference to...
  • With this letter I hereby would like to...
  • I should also like to point out that...
  • I would be grateful if you could (kindly)...
  • Should you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.
  • I look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.
  • Yours sincerely (if you know the name of the recipient).
  • Yours faithfully (if you don’t know the name of the recipient).
